Formula for converting Celsius to Fahrenheit

Temperature S in Fahrenheit (°F) is equal to the temperature S in Celsius (°C) multiplied by 9/5 and then added by 32.

S(°F) = (S(°C) x 9/5) + 32 or S(°F) = (S(°C) x 1.8) + 32
